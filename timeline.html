Timeline

Here is a timeline of the life of Jesus Christ. It starts with his birth and goes until his resurrection. There are bible videos for each of the corresponding timeline events. (not all events are included)

Jesus is born
Jesus is baptized by John
Jesus calls his apostles
Jesus feeds the 5000
Jesus walks on water
Jesus gives the parable of the good samaritan
Jesus gives the parable of the laborers in the vineyard
Jesus raises Lazarus from the dead
Jesus performs the Last Supper
Jesus suffers in Gethsemane
Jesus is crucified
Jesus is resurrected
